Ariel & Assaf Mazor (co-founders).

We started Next Tutor during our high school years. Like many other students, we took private lessons from time to time. The tutors were all excellent, but the learning experience was quite outdated. We decided to change that and create a modern platform that has everything tutors and their students need .

Since the launch we have come a long way. We added many interesting features. We did it hand in hand with you, the private tutors, while listening to your needs and comments. We see you as full partners and believe that this is the right way to build a product that can change and influence.

Our mission is to be helpful to as many private tutors as possible all over the world. We are sure that we have the right tools to do it, but we are not resting for a moment and are working hard to be better and better.
