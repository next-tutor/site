We are NextTutor
Our mission is to empower private tutors to work like true pros—no more time wasted on scheduling lessons, chasing payments, or long talks with parents. Focus on teaching. We'll handle the rest.
Ariel & Assaf Mazor (co-founders).

The idea for NextTutor came straight from experience. During our high school years, we took private lessons from time to time. The tutors were great, but the learning experience? Outdated and clunky. Despite our young age, we decided to accept the challenge and build a modern platform that gives both tutors and students everything they need to thrive.

Since then, we’ve come a long way. We didn’t just add features—we built real solutions, always alongside you, the private tutors. To us, you're not just users—you’re partners in building something bigger. We truly believe that together, we can reshape the world of private tutoring.

We have big plans for the future. First, we aim to reach as many tutors around the world as possible. To make that happen, we're working hard on translating the app into multiple languages. In addition, we plan to implement advanced, AI-driven tools that will empower you, the tutors, even further. The timelines are ambitious, but we're committed to reaching our goals—and exceeding them.
