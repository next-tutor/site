Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free version available forever?
Yes. The Free package is forever. The free package has our major features and you can use it quite a bit. Of course with a small number of students and without an image site
Does the subscription renew automatically?
Yes, the subscription renew automatically every month.
Can I cancel the subscription?
Yes. However, it should be emphasized that the subscription will only be canceled at the end of the paid month. Until then everything will work as usual.
What happens after cancelling the subscription?
You wouldn't be able to connect to the app or your website, and after a month all your data will be deleted.
Why do you need to connect a Google account?
Our server isn't storing the assignments, we use your Google Drive to save your assignments. And if you'll decide to cancel your subscription your assignments wouldn't be deleted. (Because they are yours!)
Is there any limit for the amount of lessons/assignments I can give?
There is no limit to the number of lessons and assignments you can give. On the contrary, we are here to help you administratively so that you can give as many classes and jobs as possible and maximize your profit.
