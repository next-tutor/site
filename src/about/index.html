Our Story

Next Tutor started back in high school because our founder and owner, Ariel, wanted to give tutoring lessons to lower-grade students. While he loved teaching, he quickly found himself completely overwhelmed trying to manage study schedules, log task sheets, share progress reports with parents, and collect lesson fees manually. It was an absolute logistics nightmare for a busy student.

Driven by a passion to solve this chaos, Ariel built the very first digital prototype of Next Tutor. What began as a simple, sleek scheduling script to save his own sanity rapidly caught the attention of fellow school tutors, who begged to use the system to organize their own workloads.

Today, Next Tutor has expanded from a local high school experiment into a world-class, premium logistics platform. Powered by an obsession with gorgeous design and frictionless utility, we now empower thousands of private tutors globally to save hundreds of administrative hours and scale their coaching businesses effortlessly.
