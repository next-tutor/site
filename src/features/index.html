Everything Your Tutoring Bussiness Need.

Personal website.

A personal website is a must for any private tutor who wants to reach new students. If you're interested but don't know where to start, you've come to the right place. We have a template designed especially for you, the private tutor. You are not required to do anything but insert your personal content and publish. Your website will be stored in Google's cloud to ensure maximum performance.
VIEW OUR DEMO SITE

Students Online Accounts.

Each of your students will benfit a personal account, where he can view his lessons summaries, submitted homework, paid tasks, deposits history and more. This is a truly game changer for your students (and their parents). It will change the way they learn. No more messy learning that is hard to follow, but an organized one where everything is just a click away. The results will speak for themselves.

Lessons scheduling.

Lessons requests will be waiting for you on your website. You can approve them, reject them or move them to the waiting list at your convenience. Before you approve, the student will deposit the money in his account so that you don't have to chase after your money. It's really easy and simple. Once you start you won't be able to understand how you managed before.

Tasks Handling.

Assign interactive homework, track submission statuses in real time, and review tutees' work directly from your dashboard. Next Tutor handles file attachments, grade summaries, and parent notifications automatically. Keep students accountable and engaged between lessons with a robust homework portal that prevents messy emails.

Incomes Dashboard.

Visualize your earnings, outstanding payments, and lessons records in one secure hub. See monthly analytics, track automatic deposits from client student wallets, and generate invoices with a single click. Spend less time in spreadsheets and more time teaching, while enjoying absolute financial clarity.
