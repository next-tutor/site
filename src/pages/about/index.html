We are NextTutor
Our mission is to empower private tutors to work like true pros—no more time wasted on scheduling lessons, chasing payments, or long talks with parents. Focus on teaching. We'll handle the rest.
Ariel & Assaf Mazor (co-founders).

Next Tutor was built from real experience. Back in high school, we took private lessons. The tutors were amazing — but the learning experience felt outdated and messy. So we took on the challenge: to build a modern, streamlined platform that gives tutors and students everything they need to succeed.

Since then, we've come a long way. We didn't just add features—we built real solutions, always alongside you, the private tutors. To us, you're not just users—you're partners in building something bigger. We truly believe that together, we can reshape the world of private tutoring.

We've got big plans—both near and far. Right now, we're working hard on launching our mobile app—first for Android, and then for iOS. After that, we plan to translate the platform into multiple languages, making it accessible to tutors all over the world. And of course… AI is on the way. The timeline is ambitious—but we're fully committed to making it happen.
