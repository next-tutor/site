Online Scheduling
With Next Tutor, lesson scheduling is fully online. Students can easily book sessions through their personal portal, based on your live calendar availability. For those who want recurring weekly lessons, setting up a subscription with upfront payment is quick and seamless.
Online Payments
Say goodbye to the awkwardness of chasing down payments. With Next Tutor, we’ve integrated payment directly into the booking process. Students are required to pay for their lesson upfront at the exact moment they schedule it. Only when the payment is successfully processed is the lesson officially locked into your calendar
Email & SMS Reminders
With Next Tutor, missed lessons become a thing of the past. Your students receive one or more reminders via email or SMS before each session, keeping them on time. If a student misses a lesson without canceling in advance, the payment is non-refundable.
