Increase your incomes.
Got Some Extra Time? Turn It into Value! With NextTutor, you can offer your students offline help. You decide which what to accept and at what price.
Receive Requests
Let your students send you requests for help—anything from solving exercises to writing assignments. You can choose to accept or decline each request. If you decide to proceed, you'll be asked to provide an estimated completion time and a price quote.
Wait for Payment
The student reviews your offer on his portal. If he accepts your time estimate and price, payment is required upfront (via PayPal or any provider you choose). Only then do you begin working.
Work on Your Own Time
Once payment is made, it's your turn to get to work. You can deliver the work all at once or in stages to collect feedback. Everything is clear, smooth, and hassle-free—no more back-and-forth phone calls.
